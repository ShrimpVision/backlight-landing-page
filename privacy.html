Privacy Policy

BackLight keeps your data to itself.

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

BackLight is a local macOS utility. It is designed to control a visual screen overlay on your Mac and does not require an account to use.

Data Collection

BackLight does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. The app does not include analytics, advertising, third-party trackers, or account creation.

Local Storage

BackLight may store local app preferences on your device, such as visual settings and launch preferences. This information stays on your Mac and is used only to make the app work as expected.

Permissions

BackLight may request macOS permissions needed for functionality, such as accessibility-related access used to support its overlay behavior. These permissions are handled by macOS and can be reviewed or changed in System Settings.

Third Parties

BackLight does not embed third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. If you download the app from the App Store or GitHub, those platforms may process information under their own policies.

Changes

This policy may be updated if BackLight changes in a way that affects privacy. Any updates will be posted on this page.
