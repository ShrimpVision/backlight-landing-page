for the backlightless elite

Your MacBook Neo forgot the keyboard lights. BackLight did not.

Some visionary in Cupertino made a laptop so clean and brave it forgot to light the keys. BackLight fixes that by putting a bright white bar at the bottom of your screen, so your display can literally light up your keyboard.

Download for macOS Receipts on GitHub

macOS 13+ • DMG drop imminent • App Store glow-up later

What it does

Pins a clean white strip to the bottom of the display and adds a dimmed overlay with a cursor-following spotlight.

What it really does

Turns your screen into a tiny stage light for your keyboard because the hardware team apparently chose chaos.

Why this exists

Because typing in the dark should not require a ring light, a candle, or blind faith in muscle memory.
